8 oz. Burger & Co.

Take Out Only
Wednesday To Sunday
4:00pm-9:00pm
For one of my favorite restaurants to write about is 8 oz. Burger located in the Ballard location. They also have another location that is in Capitol Hill. I've only gone here a handful of times but their food was delicious! I never had a burger so juicy and flavorful. 8 oz. Burger open in 2011 by a chef named Kevin Chung. Their house blend patties are made from naturally raised, antibiotic free, hormone free, 100% local black angus from Open Prairie located in Pasco, WA. They also committ themselvs in sourcing the best local and sustainable organic ingredients.
