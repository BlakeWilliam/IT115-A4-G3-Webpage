Humble Pie


By Rich Jensen

Take Out / Dine In / Delivery
Tuesday To Sunday
12:00pm-9:00pm
Dining at Humble Pie is an experience much richer than simply enjoying a delicious and reasonably priced pizza in a convenient and comfortable setting. The site is also a remarkable demonstration of environmentally sustainable design practices. Built from several customized shipping containers and simple wooden sheds the restaurant has the feel of a model urban mini-farm. To the side of the open-air dining area, several chickens roam freely in a large hutch eating scraps while contributing eggs to the kitchen and soil nutrients to the rooftop herb farm. The roof of the dining shed is actually a mosaic of large solar panels that provide for most of the facilityâ€™s electrical needs. Rainwater is captured and used on site for the chickens, the gardens and low-maintenance landscaping. Pizzas are baked in a traditional, wood-fired ceramic oven cast on-site and feature local, organic produce and vegan meat options. Artful salads, local craft brews and regional wines round out the offerings and drive home the point that living simply and sustainably is an act of creativity not sacrifice.
